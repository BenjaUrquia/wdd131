Site Name

Deal & Play: The name of the site has been chosen to evoke a sense of speed and simplicity, since the website is focused on teaching the rules of different games, it is intended that the user can start playing as soon as possible while understanding the rules of the game.

Site Purpose

The goal of this website is to provide users with quick access to knowledge, answers to their questions and fun with friends, demonstrating rules of your favorite games.

Scenarios

Scenario 1

Question: "How do I play poker, and are there variations of the rules we can try?"

Solution: The site provides a detailed guide on the game's rules, setup, and gameplay, along with variations to keep things interesting. Additional tips or videos might also be included for quick understanding.

Scenario 2

Question: "What are some quick and fun card games I can learn to play with my family?"

Solution: The site offers a list of card games. Each game page includes a brief description, rules and materials needed to start playing.

Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Blue (#002D62) Usage: Navigation bar background, Main headings, Footer background.

Accent Color: Golden Yellow (#F2C94C) Usage:Buttons and interactive elements. Section dividers or highlights for important text. Icons and decorative elements.

Background Color: Light Gray (#E8E8E8) Usage: General page background for improved readability and contrast with dark text.

Typography

Primary Font: Lora (Serif) Usage: Main headings (h1, h2) to give the site an elegant and professional feel.

Secondary Font: Open Sans (Sans-Serif) Usage: Body text and paragraphs for readability and simplicity. Navigation menu and footer text.
