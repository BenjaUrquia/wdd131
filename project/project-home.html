ENJOY THE BEST CARD GAMES HERE!

Find the best card games to play with your friends! We recommend our most wanted games!

  • Poker
    • Deck type: France-suited
    • Players: 2-10
    • Average duration: 30-40 minutes
    • Objective: To form the best possible hand by combining private and community cards, or to convince your opponents that you have done so through betting strategies.
  • Truco
    • Deck type: Spanish-suited
    • Players: 1-6
    • Average duration: 15-30 minutes
    • Objective: Earn points in consecutive rounds through strategic skills, bluffing and luck. The maximum score is usually 30 points, although some games are played to 15 points.
  • Black-Jack
    • Deck type: France-suited
    • Players: 1 - 7 vs croupier
    • Average duration: 5-15 minutes per round
    • Objective: Achieve a score of 21 or as close to 21 as possible without going over, competing against the dealer. If the player or the dealer goes over 21, they automatically lose.
